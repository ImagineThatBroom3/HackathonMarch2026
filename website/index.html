About ▶

FitQuest Journey is a fitness resource for seniors. It uses a baseline test to determine a personalized safe and healthy workout.

! Reminder !

Remember to stay safe! Take regular breaks for water, and don't overwork yourself!

If you start feeling faint or dizzy, sit down - don't wait to finish that last rep!

If you have arthritis or any other problematic medical conditions, please check with your doctor before using this app!
